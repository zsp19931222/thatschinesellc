Culture Event

Besides language courses and workshops, we also organize cultural events to help Chinese learners understand how profound Chinese culture is. To date, we have had over 50 events with up to 1000 people in total.

SHADOW PUPPETS

Understand the history and development of shadow puppets. Watch shadow puppets. Hand-draw and assemble shadow puppets. Shadow puppet freestyle performance.

CHINESE PAINTING

Understand the four treasures of the study: pen,ink, paper and inkstone. Introduce the characteristics of Traditional Chinese painting, ink and color techniques. Imitate and practice drawing bamboo. Make a painting and take it home.

HANDMADE RING

Learn about Chinese silver jewelry. Measure finger size and polish the silver bar. Carve your favorite pattern on the silver bar. Fire silver bars, bent to suitable size.

THE DRAGON BOATFESTIVAL

Learn about the origins and customs. Learn vocabulary and expressions. Learn how to make zongzi. Boil rice dumplings and taste them, drink beer and play games.

SICHUAN CUISINE CLASS

Local food market visiting & experience. Learn to cook most popular Sichuan food. Learn Chinese food culture and more stories behind. Enjoy the food you made and get the recipes.

LESHAN ONE-DAY TOUR

Take a cruise to see the Giant Buddha in Leshan and learn about its history. Visit local ancient towns, traditional siheyuan and handicraft workshops. Taste Leshan local snacks. Go to the tea mountain and learn how to pick and make tea.
